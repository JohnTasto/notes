# Rationals

## Rules

\[
  \begin{aligned}
    a^{-1} &= \pc{1 \/ a^n}{1 \/ a  }                           \h7 \\
    a^{-n} &=              {1 \/ a^n}                           \h7 \\[1em]
    \({\vp{b}a \/ b})^{-\pc{n}1} &= \pc{\({b \/ a})}{b \/ a}    \h7 \\
    \({\vp{b}a \/ b})^{-      n} &=               \({b \/ a})^n \h7 \\[1em]
    \vc{{      1 \/ {a \/ b}}} &= \pc{cb \/ a }{ b \/ a }       \h7 \\
    \vc{{\vp{1}c \/ {a \/ b}}} &=              {cb \/ a }       \h7 \\
    \vc{     {{a \/ b} \/ c }} &=              {a  \/ cb}       \h7 \\
  \end{aligned}
\]
