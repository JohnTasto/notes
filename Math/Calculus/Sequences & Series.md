# Sequences & Series

## Arithmetic Sequence

\[
  \begin{matrix}
    a_n = a_1 + d(n - 1) \\[.5em]
    \text{where} \\[.5em]
    \begin{aligned}
      a_n &= \text{term at position } n \\
      a_1 &= \text{first term} \\
        n &= \text{term position} \\
        d &= \text{common difference between terms}
    \end{aligned}
  \end{matrix}
\]
